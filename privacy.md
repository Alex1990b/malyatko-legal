# Privacy Policy for “Malyatko”

_Last updated: February 1, 2026_

## Introduction

“Malyatko” (“we”, “us”, “our”) is a mobile application designed to help parents and caregivers track and organize information related to their child’s daily activities, development, and routines.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and handled when you use the app.

This Privacy Policy applies to all users, regardless of the app language.

---

## Information We Collect

### Data You Provide

The app allows you to create child profiles and store information such as:

- Child-related records and logs (entered by you)
- Reminders and notes
- Activity and development tracking entries

All such data is created and controlled by you.

---

### Analytics Data

We use **Firebase Analytics** to collect **anonymous usage analytics**, including:

- App feature usage
- Screen views
- Actions such as adding or deleting records
- Aggregated counts and non-identifying flags

**We do not collect or send** the following data to analytics services:

- Child names
- Dates of birth
- Photos
- Health measurements
- Medical records
- Any personally identifiable information

---

## How We Use Information

We use information only to:

- Provide and operate app functionality
- Improve app stability and user experience
- Understand feature usage in aggregate
- Deliver reminders and notifications (if enabled)

We do not sell or share personal data with third parties.

---

## Data Storage and Security

- App data is stored locally on your device
- Optional backups and synchronization may use **Apple iCloud**, if enabled by you
- We do not operate our own servers for storing personal or child-related data

---

## Data Deletion

You may:

- Delete records or child profiles directly within the app
- Remove local data by deleting the app
- Manage iCloud backups through your Apple ID settings

---

## Children’s Privacy

“Malyatko” is intended to be used by **parents or legal guardians**.  
We do not knowingly collect personal data directly from children.

---

## Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party service:

- **Firebase Analytics (Google)** — anonymous usage analytics only

---

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.  
Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
